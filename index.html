Universidade Tecnológica Federal do Paraná (UTFPR)

UTFPR is one of the most renowned Universities in Brazil. Their teaching pattern is inspired by the European polytechnic university model. It specializes in natural sciences and engineering.

Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina (UFSC)

The history of the Federal University of Santa Catarina has its roots in the Polytechnic Institute of Florianópolis founded on 13 March of 1917 by José Arthur Boiteux.

Departamento Acadêmico de Eletrotécnica (DAELT)

Academic Department of Electrotechnics at the Curitiba Campus of UTFPR is responsible for organizing teaching, research, and extension activities in the field of Electrical and Control Engineering.
